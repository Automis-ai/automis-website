AI automations connect your CRM, calendar, ads, and communication tools into a seamless system. Instead of relying on staff to follow up manually, Automis builds AI-driven workflows that qualify leads, book appointments, and send reminders automatically. Businesses save hours every week, reduce human error, and scale faster with AI-powered efficiency. For appointment-based businesses, it's the difference between lost leads and steady growth.

## What Are AI Automations?

AI automations are smart workflows that replace repetitive manual tasks with machine-driven processes. Instead of staff manually copying leads, sending reminders, and updating CRMs, automations do all of it instantly and reliably. Automis builds end-to-end systems linking ads, CRM, calendar, and communication tools so no lead is wasted.

## Why Businesses Need Automations

- **Speed is everything**: 70% of leads go cold if not contacted within 5 minutes
- **Staff workload is costly**: 10–20 hours/week wasted on admin
- **Consistency wins**: AI ensures every lead gets the same fast, high-quality experience

## How AI Automations Work (Step-by-Step)

1. Lead captured from ads/forms
2. Instant qualification by AI receptionist or chatbot
3. CRM sync with HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel
4. Booking into calendar
5. Reminders via SMS, email, WhatsApp
6. Follow-up sequences until conversion
